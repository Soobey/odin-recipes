Pizza

Description

Do you like pizza? Of course you do! Here is an easy recipe to make a delicious pizza at home.

Ingredients

  • 1.25 cups warm water
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 1.5 teaspoon fine salt
  • 0.25 cup olive oil, divided
  • 1 (28 ounce) can plain crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (14 ounce) can pizza sauce (such as Don Peppion)
  • 8 ounces low-moisture whole-milk mozzarella, very thinly sliced
  • 0.25 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
