Muffin

Description

Do you want to make your family or friends happy? Than you have to cook these muffins for them! It's sweet and easy to make. It it possible to make them in under one hour so it's not really time consuming! Just try them!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup light cream
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 0.5 cup quick-cooking oats
  • 0.5 cup fresh blueberries
