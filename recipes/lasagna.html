Lasagna

Description

An easy to make meal! The preparation needs only 20 minutes of your time and you can put it in the oven! Afer about one hour it's done and you able to serve it.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1/4-inch strips
  • 0.5 cup lime juice
  • 2 (1 ounce) packets taco seasoning mix (such as Old El Paso)
  • 1.5 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup salsa
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (8 ounce) package no-boil lasagna noodles, or to taste
